What is an Algorithm?

Algorithms sound scary, but they're just step-by-step instructions - like following a recipe to bake a cake.

An algorithm is a set of instructions to solve a problem or complete a task. Every time you follow a recipe, assemble IKEA furniture, or use GPS directions, you're following an algorithm.

The Cake Recipe Analogy

Baking a chocolate cake is an algorithm:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder
  3. Add eggs and milk
  4. Pour batter into pan
  5. Bake for 30 minutes
  6. Let cool, then serve

Follow these steps in order, and you get a cake every time!
