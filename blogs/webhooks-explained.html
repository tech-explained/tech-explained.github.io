ğŸ”” The Doorbell Analogy

Without webhooks: Check door every minute (polling). With webhooks: Doorbell rings when someone arrives (event-driven). Much more efficient!

How Webhooks Work

  1. You register a URL with a service
  2. Event happens (payment, signup, etc.)
  3. Service sends HTTP POST to your URL
  4. Your app receives and processes data

Polling vs Webhooks

Polling (Old Way):

  • Check for updates repeatedly
  • Wastes resources
  • Delayed notifications

Webhooks (Modern):

  • Get notified immediately
  • Efficient
  • Real-time updates
